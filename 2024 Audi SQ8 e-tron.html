2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron

2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron

Price Range (starting): $90 995 or $93 795



Performance and Powertrain

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron is powered by a three-motor electric drivetrain, offering a total system output of 496 horsepower and 717 lb-ft of torque. This configuration includes two rear electric motors for precise torque vectoring, enhancing agility and handling.

  • Acceleration: 0 to 60 mph in 4.5 seconds.

  • Drive Type: Quattro all-wheel drive (electric version).

  • Transmission: Single-speed automatic for smooth and instantaneous power delivery.

  • Driving Dynamics: Features air suspension and an advanced chassis for a comfortable yet sporty ride, adaptable for urban roads and challenging terrains.


Battery and Range

The SQ8 e-tron comes equipped with a 114 kWh lithium-ion battery pack (usable capacity), which offers competitive range and energy efficiency.

  • Range: Estimated 240-253 miles depending on the variant and driving conditions.

  • Energy Consumption: Approximately 44 kWh per 100 miles under standard conditions.

  • Efficiency Modes: Multiple driving modes (efficiency, comfort, dynamic, and individual) optimize battery usage and performance.


Charging Capabilities

Charging is both fast and convenient:

  • DC Fast Charging: Supports up to 170 kW, enabling an 80% charge in approximately 31 minutes.

  • AC Charging: Compatible with 11 kW or optional 22 kW AC chargers for at-home or public charging stations.

  • Plug Type: CCS (Combined Charging System).



Interior and Comfort

The cabin of the SQ8 e-tron is designed with premium materials and cutting-edge features:

  • Seats: Leather-upholstered, power-adjustable heated seats with ventilation and massage functions in the front.

  • Space: Ample legroom and headroom for all passengers, offering class-leading comfort.

  • Ambient Lighting: 30-color customizable LED ambient lighting enhances the luxurious feel.

  • Cargo Capacity: Offers 24.6 cubic feet of cargo space behind the rear seats, expanding to 56.5 cubic feet when folded.


Technology and Infotainment

The SQ8 e-tron integrates Audi’s latest MMI technology:

  • Displays:

    • 12.3-inch Virtual Cockpit for driver information.

    • 10.1-inch upper touchscreen for navigation and media.

    • 8.6-inch lower touchscreen for climate controls and seat settings.


  • Connectivity: Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, along with Amazon Alexa integration.

  • Audio System: Bang & Olufsen premium 16-speaker surround sound.


Safety and Driver Assistance

The SQ8 e-tron includes an advanced suite of driver aids, branded as Audi Pre Sense:

  • Standard Features:

    • Adaptive cruise control with lane-keeping assist.

    • Automatic emergency braking and pedestrian detection.

    • Blind-spot monitoring with rear cross-traffic alert.


  • Optional Upgrades: Remote parking assist and a 360-degree surround-view camera system.


Exterior Design

The exterior reflects Audi's bold design language, with sporty and futuristic touches:

  • Lighting: HD Matrix LED headlights and LED taillights with dynamic indicators.

  • Wheels: Standard 20-inch alloy wheels, with optional 22-inch wheels available.

  • Aerodynamics: Active grille shutters and air curtains improve efficiency and reduce drag.

  • Color Options: Multiple metallic and matte finishes, including an exclusive Daytona Gray and Navarra Blue.


Pricing and Variants

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron is available in two body styles:

  1. Standard SUV: Starts at $90 995.

  2. Sportback (Coupe-like design): Starts at $93 795.

Additional packages and features can increase the price significantly.


Driving Experience

The SQ8 e-tron offers an engaging and balanced driving experience:

  • Steering: Precise and responsive, with adjustable weighting based on driving mode.

  • Ride Comfort: Adaptive air suspension smoothens road imperfections, while dynamic mode sharpens handling.

  • Off-Road Capability: Although primarily a luxury SUV, it retains light off-road functionality with adjustable ride height.


Sustainability Features

Audi emphasizes sustainability with the SQ8 e-tron:

  • Materials: Recycled materials in interior trims and carpets.

  • Carbon Neutrality: Audi claims the production process is carbon-neutral.


The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron is a luxurious and high-performing electric SUV that blends cutting-edge technology with everyday practicality. Its three-motor configuration delivers exceptional power, while its advanced infotainment and safety systems ensure comfort and peace of mind. Perfect for those seeking a premium EV without compromising performance or style.
