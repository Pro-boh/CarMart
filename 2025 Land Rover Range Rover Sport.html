2025 Land Rover Range Rover Sport



2024 Honda Prologue

Price Range (starting): $89 000



The 2025 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury midsize SUV that strikes a perfect balance between sporty performance, luxurious comfort, and off-road prowess. As a slightly smaller sibling of the flagship Range Rover, the Sport model caters to buyers seeking a dynamic driving experience paired with premium amenities. With its refined design, electrified powertrain options, and advanced off-road technology, it solidifies its position as a standout in the luxury SUV market.


Exterior Design

  • Key Highlights:

    • Sleek and sporty profile with bold lines and a muscular stance.

    • Flush door handles and a minimalist grille for a modern aesthetic.

    • Matrix LED headlights with signature DRL design.

    • Aerodynamic enhancements like roof spoilers and air curtains.


  • Dimensions:

    • Length: 193.5 inches

    • Width: 81.6 inches (including mirrors)

    • Height: 71 inches

    • Wheelbase: 118 inches


  • Paint and Trim Options:

    • Available in a range of premium finishes, including Santorini Black, Fuji White, and Sunset Gold Satin.

    • Gloss black exterior trim packages for a sportier appearance.



Interior Features

  • Seating and Comfort:

    • Seating for up to 5 passengers with generous legroom and headroom.

    • Premium leather upholstery with options for semi-aniline or sustainable materials like Kvadrat wool blend.

    • Massaging, heated, and ventilated front seats.


  • Technology and Infotainment:

    • 13.1-inch Pivi Pro touchscreen system with over-the-air updates.

    • Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto integration.

    • Meridian Signature Sound System with 29 speakers and 1,430 watts of power.

    • Rear-seat entertainment with dual 11.4-inch screens (optional).


  • Practicality:

    • Cargo Space: 31.9 cubic feet (seats up) and 118 cubic feet (seats folded).

    • Configurable interior lighting with 30-color options.

    • Ample storage compartments, including a split glove box and center console refrigerator.



Performance

  • Powertrain Options:

    • Standard Engine: 3.0L inline-6 turbocharged with mild-hybrid assistance (395 hp).

    • Plug-In Hybrid: 3.0L inline-6 combined with a 105 kW electric motor (434 hp, 48-mile electric range).

    • High-Performance Model (SVR): 4.4L twin-turbo V8 (615 hp).


  • Transmission and Drivetrain:

    • 8-speed automatic transmission.

    • Standard all-wheel-drive with Intelligent Driveline Dynamics for optimal traction.


  • Driving Dynamics:

    • Adaptive air suspension for a smooth ride on various terrains.

    • Dynamic Response Pro for enhanced cornering stability.

    • Configurable drive modes including Eco, Comfort, Dynamic, and Off-Road.



Off-Road Capabilities

  • Ground Clearance: Adjustable up to 11.6 inches.

  • Wading Depth: Capable of fording water up to 35.4 inches.

  • Terrain Response 2 System: Automatically adjusts settings for different surfaces, including mud, sand, and snow.

  • All-Terrain Progress Control: Low-speed cruise control for challenging off-road conditions.


Safety and Driver Assistance

  • Standard Safety Features:

    • Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop & Go.

    • Lane Keep Assist and Blind Spot Monitoring.

    • Autonomous Emergency Braking.

    • Rear Traffic Collision Alert.


  • Advanced Assistance Systems:

    • ClearSight Ground View for a transparent hood effect during off-roading.

    • 3D surround-view camera for parking and maneuvering.

    • Driver Condition Monitor to detect fatigue.



Electrification Features

  • Plug-in hybrid offers up to 48 miles of electric-only range.

  • Regenerative braking to recover energy during deceleration.

  • Supports DC fast charging with up to 80% charge in 30 minutes (50 kW).


Fuel Economy and Efficiency

  • Mild-Hybrid Engine: 20 MPG city / 25 MPG highway.

  • Plug-In Hybrid (combined): ~50 MPGe.

  • V8 Model: 16 MPG city / 21 MPG highway.


Trim Levels and Pricing

  • Available Trims:

    1. SE: Starting at $89 000 – Standard luxury features and mild-hybrid powertrain.

    2. Dynamic SE: Starting at $95 000 – Enhanced sport styling and additional performance features.

    3. Autobiography: Starting at $108 000 – Ultimate luxury with semi-aniline leather and exclusive options.

    4. SVR (High Performance): Starting at $130 000 – Twin-turbo V8 and aggressive styling cues.


  • Customization Options:

    • Exclusive wheel designs ranging from 20 to 23 inches.

    • Bespoke interior combinations through Land Rover’s SV Bespoke program.



Competitors

  • Key Rivals:

    • Porsche Cayenne: More focused on sporty handling but less spacious.

    • BMW X5 M50i: Offers a balance of luxury and performance at a similar price.

    • Mercedes-Benz GLE-Class: Slightly softer ride with comparable luxury features.

    • Tesla Model X: EV alternative with advanced tech but less off-road capability.



Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Strengths:

    • Unmatched off-road capabilities in its class.

    • Luxurious and spacious interior with cutting-edge technology.

    • Wide range of powertrains, including eco-friendly plug-in hybrid and high-performance V8.


  • Weaknesses:

    • Premium pricing, especially for higher trims.

    • Fuel economy is less competitive in the non-hybrid models.

    • Infotainment system, while improved, may still have a slight learning curve.



Availability

The 2024 Honda Prologue is expected to arrive at dealerships in early 2024. The 2025 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is available for order in early 2024, with deliveries expected by mid-2024. Buyers can configure their vehicle through Land Rover dealerships or the official website.
