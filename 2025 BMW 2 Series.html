2025 BMW 2 Series



2025 BMW 2 SERIES

Price Range: $39 200 - $52 100



Engine Options and Performance

  • 230i Coupe: Powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine, it produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The 230i accelerates from 0-60 mph in 5.5 seconds and achieves up to 26 mpg city and 35 mpg highway.

  • M240i Coupe: This model comes with a more powerful 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine, delivering 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It reaches 0-60 mph in just 4.1 seconds. The M240i xDrive offers improved acceleration with a 5.3-second 0-60 time. Fuel economy is estimated at 23 mpg city and 32 mpg highway.

  • M2: M2: For enthusiasts seeking ultimate performance, the M2 is equipped with a 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine generating 473 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in 3.9 seconds and delivers a thrilling driving experience with precise handling and a focus on sportiness.


Design and Exterior

  • Dimensions: The 2025 2 Series Coupe has a compact footprint, offering sporty proportions that include a wider stance and lower roofline compared to its predecessor.

  • Front Fascia: The signature BMW kidney grille is prominently featured, with illuminated options available on higher trims.

  • Aerodynamics: The car’s aggressive design is coupled with performance-enhancing aerodynamics, contributing to its stability at higher speeds and its overall driving dynamics.

  • Wheel Options: Standard wheels range from 17 inches on the 230i to 19 inches on the M240i.


Interior and Features

  • Technology: The 2 Series comes equipped with a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 14.9-inch central display for infotainment, featuring the latest BMW iDrive 8 system.

  • Seating and Comfort: Standard sport seats with adjustable bolsters enhance comfort and support. Optional upgrades include M Sport leather upholstery and heated/ventilated seats.

  • Cabin Materials: The interior combines high-quality materials, including leather-wrapped surfaces, M-specific trims, and ambient lighting to create a luxurious atmosphere.

  • Infotainment: Standard features include Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and wireless charging. Optional systems include a premium sound system by Harman Kardon.


Safety Features

  • The 2025 BMW 2 Series is equipped with a comprehensive suite of safety features, including:

    • Active Blind Spot Detection

    • Lane Departure Warning

    • Forward Collision Warning

    • Automatic Emergency Braking

    • Rearview Camera and Parking Sensors

    • Adaptive Cruise Control


  • BMW’s Driving Assistance Professional Package adds additional features like Extended Traffic Jam Assistant and Automatic Lane Change for added convenience.


Driving Experience

  • Handling and Suspension: The 2 Series offers a sporty driving experience, with responsive steering and an emphasis on dynamic handling. The M240i features an upgraded adaptive suspension for a balance of comfort and performance.

  • All-Wheel Drive (xDrive): Available on both the 230i and M240i, the xDrive system enhances traction, especially in challenging weather conditions, providing superior grip on both wet and dry roads.


Fuel Economy and Efficiency

  • 230i Coupe: The fuel economy is estimated at 26 mpg city and 35 mpg highway, which is competitive within its class.

  • M240i Coupe: This model achieves 23 mpg city and 32 mpg highway, providing a solid balance of power and efficiency.

  • M2: While more focused on performance, the M2 still offers reasonable fuel efficiency given its performance-oriented nature, with estimates of 19 mpg city and 26 mpg highway.


Pricing and Trims

  • The 230i Coupe starts around $40,375 with RWD and can be upgraded to xDrive for enhanced traction.

  • The M240i is priced starting at $47,595 and offers more aggressive styling, higher performance, and enhanced features compared to the 230i.

  • The M2:starts around $63,000 and provides the highest performance, handling, and styling cues tailored for driving enthusiasts.


Competitors

  • The BMW 2 Series competes with models like the Audi S3, Mercedes-Benz CLA-Class, and the Genesis G70, offering a unique combination of sporty dynamics, luxury features, and performance.
