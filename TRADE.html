Trade or Sell Your Car with Confidence

Turn your current car into your next opportunity. At CarMart, we make trading in or selling your vehicle simple, transparent, and rewarding.

How It Works

  1. Tell us about your car – share basic details like make, model, year, mileage, and condition.
  2. Get a fair estimate – we evaluate current market value and provide a competitive offer.
  3. Bring your car in – visit one of our locations for a quick inspection and final confirmation.
  4. Choose cash or trade-in credit – apply your car’s value toward a new vehicle or receive payment.
